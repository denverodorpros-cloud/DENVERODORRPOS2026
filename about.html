About us

We do not mask odors. We help remove them at the source.

Denver Odor Pros provides professional ozone odor removal for Denver homes, cars, apartments, rental turnovers, and real estate listings.

Local-service focused odor removal

Stubborn odors can settle into carpet, upholstery, drywall, cabinets, vents, subfloors, and vehicle interiors. Our work starts with the source, then uses professional ozone treatment and odor-neutralizing steps to improve the space without relying on cover-up fragrances.

We work with homeowners, renters, landlords, property managers, real estate agents, hosts, car owners, and businesses across Denver and nearby Colorado Front Range cities.

Common projects

  • Pet urine and animal odors in homes and rentals
  • Smoke odor removal in apartments, houses, and vehicles
  • Marijuana odor removal before move-out, sale, or new tenants
  • Mold and musty odor treatment after moisture issues are addressed
  • Vehicle odor removal for smoke, pet, mildew, and resale problems
  • Real estate odor removal before photos, showings, and open houses
