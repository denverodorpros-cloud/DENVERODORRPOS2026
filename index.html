Ozone treatment process

We neutralize odors instead of covering them up.

Ozone (O3) reacts with odor molecules in air, fabrics, vents, cracks, and porous surfaces. The space stays unoccupied during treatment, then is ventilated before use.

1

Inspect

Identify odor source, affected rooms, materials, and access needs.

2

Prepare

Remove or treat source material so ozone can reach the odor pathway.

3

Treat

Run professional ozone equipment in a sealed, unoccupied space.

4

Ventilate

Air out the space and confirm it is ready for normal use.
